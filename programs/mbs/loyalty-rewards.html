    It's That Simple

    Cash in your points earned for a wide selection of merchandise in the on-line award catalog.

    Account Status

    If applicable, your MBS credit account must remain current and in good standing in order to receive award points. If your MBS account is not current at the time of points load, your points earned for the month will be surrendered and will not be reinstated. Your Loyalty Rewards account will be put on credit hold if the MBS account is not current and will remain on hold until payment is received.

    Qualifying Material Purchases

    Material total of invoice only applies toward points quota. Sales tax, delivery charges and discounts are not included in point totals. All types of materials purchased will apply. Program rules are based on purchases shipped from our warehouse stock.

    Obligation/Affiliation

    Recipients assume any tax liability for rewards earned. MBS must receive a completed W-9 form prior to activating your Loyalty Rewards account.

    How To Earn Points

    Points are accumulated on purchases made at Modern Builders Supply January though December. In order to see if you qualify for the Loyalty Rewards Program, contact your local branch.

    How To Redeem Points

    All redemptions must be made online at www.awardllnk.com. For security reasons you must call the MBS Promotions Department (800.416.8013), your MBS Representative or MBS Branch Manager for your pin and account number. This information will be used to login to your rewards account.

    Points Expiration

    The deadline to redeem your points is January 31st of the following year. Unused points will not be carried over from year to year and do not have any cash value.

    Availability of Products

    Modern Builders Supply is not responsible for availability of award merchandise. No cash buy-ups will be permitted.
